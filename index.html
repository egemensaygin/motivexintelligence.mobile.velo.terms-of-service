Privacy Policy

Effective date: April 6, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Motivex Intelligence (“we”, “us”, or “our”) collects, uses, and shares information when you use the Velo mobile application (the “App”). By using Velo you agree to the practices described in this policy.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following categories of information:

a) Health & Fitness Data

With your explicit permission, Velo reads health and fitness data from Apple HealthKit (iOS) or Google Health Connect (Android). This may include:

  • Step count, distance walked/run, active energy burned, exercise minutes
  • Heart rate, resting heart rate, heart rate variability (HRV)
  • Sleep analysis (duration, stages)
  • Body metrics (weight, BMI) — only if you enter them
  • Workout sessions and activity types
  • Hydration / water intake you log manually in the App

Important: Health data is processed locally on your device whenever possible. Summary aggregates (e.g., daily step counts, weekly readiness scores) may be stored in your personal cloud account to enable sync across your devices. Your raw sensor data is never sold or shared with advertisers.

b) Account Information

When you create a Velo account we collect your email address, display name, and a profile photo (optional). Authentication is handled by Firebase Authentication (Google LLC).

c) Usage & Analytics Data

We use Firebase Analytics and Firebase Crashlytics to understand how the App is used and to diagnose crashes. These tools may collect:

  • Device model, OS version, app version
  • Screen views and feature interactions (anonymized)
  • Crash reports and stack traces
  • Approximate country / locale (not precise location)

Analytics data is aggregated and does not include your health data.

d) Social Features Data

If you use leaderboards, challenges, or friend features, we store the display name, profile photo, and fitness summary scores (e.g., weekly steps rank) necessary to operate those features in Firestore. You can disable social features at any time in Settings.

Purpose Data Used

We do not use your data for targeted advertising.

3. Health Data & Platform Rules

Apple HealthKit: Data obtained from Apple HealthKit is used solely to provide and improve health-related features within Velo. We do not use HealthKit data for advertising, or share it with third parties except as required to operate the App (e.g., secure cloud sync). We comply fully with Apple’s HealthKit data use requirements.

Google Health Connect: Data obtained from Health Connect is used exclusively to power the features you enable in the App. We comply with Google’s Health Connect permissions policy and do not use this data for purposes beyond those disclosed here.

4. Data Sharing & Third Parties

We share data only in the following limited circumstances:

  • Service providers: Google Firebase (authentication, database, analytics, crash reporting). These providers act as data processors under appropriate data-processing agreements.
  • Social features: If you participate in leaderboards or challenges, your display name and activity summary are visible to other users you choose to connect with.
  • Legal requirements: We may disclose information if required by law, court order, or to protect rights and safety.
  • Business transfers: If Motivex Intelligence is acquired or merges with another company, your data may be transferred as part of that transaction. We will notify you before your data becomes subject to a different privacy policy.

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal or health data with data brokers or advertisers.

5. Data Retention

We retain your account and cloud-synced data for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, all personal data is deleted from our servers within 30 days, except where we are required by law to retain it longer.

Anonymized, aggregated analytics data (which cannot identify you) may be retained indefinitely to improve the App.

6. Your Rights & Choices

  • Access & portability: You can export your data from the App at any time (Profile → Settings → Export Data).
  • Correction: You can update your profile information directly in the App.
  • Deletion: You can delete your account from Profile → Settings → Delete Account. This removes all your data from our servers.
  • Health permissions: You can revoke health data access at any time through your device’s Health app (iOS) or Health Connect (Android) settings.
  • Notifications: You can disable push notifications from your device’s system settings.
  • Social features: You can disable leaderboards and friend features in App Settings → Privacy.

If you are located in the European Economic Area (EEA), United Kingdom, or California, you may have additional rights under GDPR or CCPA. To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at the address below.

7. Children’s Privacy

Velo is not directed to children under the age of 13 (or under 16 in the EEA). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately and we will delete it.

8. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures including TLS encryption in transit, Firebase Security Rules for Firestore access control, and Firebase Authentication for identity verification. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

9. International Data Transfers

Your data is processed and stored on Google Cloud servers, which may be located outside your country of residence. Google maintains appropriate safeguards (including Standard Contractual Clauses for EEA data transfers). By using Velo you consent to this transfer.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the effective date at the top and notify you via an in-app notification or email for material changes. Your continued use of the App after changes are posted constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
